What is Fairway Finder?

Fairway Finder is a web application designed to help golfers easily discover golf courses around Nova Scotia. Whether you're new to golf or a seasoned player, Fairway Finder provides an interactive map, course details, and filters to help you find the perfect course for your needs.

Inspiration Behind the App

The inspiration for Fairway Finder came from the frustration of manually researching golf courses across Nova Scotia. Golfers, especially newcomers, often face difficulties in discovering courses without extensive searchesâ€”Googling, calling, and asking questions. It's even more challenging when you arrive at a course and find out it's beyond your budget.

Our Goals

  • Create a centralized platform for finding and comparing golf courses in Nova Scotia.
  • Provide up-to-date course information, such as pricing, hole count, and reviews.
  • Make it easy for new golfers to navigate and choose courses that fit their budget and preferences.

Future Goals

  • Expand course coverage to include golf courses outside of Nova Scotia.
  • Introduce user accounts for personalized course tracking and review submissions.
  • Integrate booking functionality so golfers can reserve tee times directly from the app.
